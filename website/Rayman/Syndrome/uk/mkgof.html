Cheats and Hints 
  1995. Michel ANCEL, a young graphic artist at UBI SOFT is working on a new animation techniques. An armless and legless hero is the result of these researches. This character progressively comes to life and gains real personality. Michel puts energy and humour into Rayman. He then convinces UBI SOFT that Rayman deserves his own world and he starts making a game out of it: thus Rayman is born!
