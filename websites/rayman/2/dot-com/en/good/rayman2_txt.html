A gang of unscrupulous pirates land in Rayman's world. Their mission is to capture and take over the population in order to sell them to a galactic circus. Rayman's first mission is to free Ly the fairy (an intriguing feline creature) who informs our hero of the only way to chase away the pirates: wake up Polochus the Magician, the world's creator and conscience.
